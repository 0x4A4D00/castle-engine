Translation (to English and to pasdoc) state of my units:

------------------------------------------------------------
"D" means "Done" means that it is in English and processed by pasdoc,
and once @section and @groupBegin/End/With will be implemented in pasdoc,
all will be perfect.

"|" means "abstract done" -- unit's abstract and description is "done"
(in English, and pasdoc-compat once @section and @groupBegin/End/With
will be implemented).

"-" means none of the above...

"E" means "english done, pasdoc doesn't process it",
    this is usually for translations of C bindings, no point in making
    them perfect for pasdoc processing.
